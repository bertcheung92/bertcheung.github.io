Team Zenko Go

Team Zenko Go is a preschool computer animated children's streaming television series produced by DreamWorks Animation Television and Mainframe Studios.
Junior Layout Artist
My Role: Set Dressing, Camera Placement and Movement, Character Blocking

After my work as a CG Production Assistant Mainframe Studios signed me on as a Junior Layout Artist to help with Team Zenko Go. This was my first production and I learned a lot. I worked mostly with static shots and focused on set dressing, composition, blocking and camera framing according to the storyboards. I did simple stepped keyframe poses for the characters.

After working on Team Zenko Go I was signed on for their currently unannounced project.
